Shri Parmar Ravi Manubhai, I.A.S. (Retd.)

Chairman (BPSC, Patna)

Shri Ravi Parmar joined the Indian Administrative Service (IAS) in 1992. Prior to this, he was also selected for Indian Police Service (IPS) in 1991 and was allotted Gujarat Cadre. He has done Masters in Public Policy from Maxwell School of Citizenship and Public Affairs, Syracuse University, New York, USA.

Prior to joining the IAS Shri Parmar served with Bank of India, State Bank of India and Customs & Excise department in various capacities. In March 2024 he took VRS to join as Chairman of Bihar Public Service Commission.

During his illustrious career, Shri Parmar has held many important positions in several departments including Health & Medical Education, Tourism, Mines & Geology, Transport, Welfare etc. in the State Government and in ministries of Steel, Personnel, Home Affairs, Shipping and Small-scale Industries in Government of India.
